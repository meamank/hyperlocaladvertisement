Hyper – Local Advertisement

 

Google MAPS API  helps to connect with consumers and shop owners through a real-time based notification system. It can be used to show different deals, announcements, events in the proximate range of your current location.

More
  • By this platform, shop owners won’t have to pay hefty prices for their ads to reach to their targeted people.
  • Users/Consumers won’t have to face any hassle of going through all the outlets of a Mall/Market to check for deals/offers.

Scope

The new standard in digital advertisements

The purpose of Hyper-local advertisement is to provide a complete location based advertising platform where users will get notified of offers when they visit their local Mall/Market. Our approach is not to fill up the notification area with the ads from all the over the city but only from the location they are currently in and if they want to see ads at that time. They can also see current offers available at a specific area.

The idea is that offers/deals will be posted by the shop owners, event organizers and users get notified when they are in the proximate range of that shop owners location or events happening nearby.
